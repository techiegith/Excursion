Top 10 places to be visited in india during winter

Embrace the glittery snow and crispy mountain breez.. There are so many destinations in India where the crispy and chilled breeze, glittering snow and enchanting mist will be bound with a tyrant spell from which you will never be able to escape. Images of glassy ponds, snowy roofs and mountains are like nectar which tourists will drink with ecstasy. Winter is the best time to explore the beautiful vistas of mountains,

1. Goa

Enjoy The Vibes of Christmas Carnival and New Year Eve!

Loud night parties, crazy crowd and delectable dishes, everything that defines an electrifying atmosphere is available in Goa. It is one of the most visited winter destinations in India. With Christmas Carnival and New Year Eve around the corner, the high spirits of Goa are best displayed in winters. Be it a bash alfresco at the beach or a high-profile get-together in a restaurant, exuberance can be seen in every nook and corner of Goa. If you want to explore something unique than beaches in Goa then check out the gorgeous spice plantations in Goa. Walking through the lush green plantations is one of the best things to do in Goa.A beautiful sunset cruise in the Mandovi River will make your day.Hopping on Islands and pristine beaches of Goa is a must not miss.Explore Goan caves which are of great historical and natural importance

2. Shimla:

The cosy feel provided by the chilled weather turns the atmosphere romantic. So, if you are looking for a romantic winter getaway, visit Shimla. This city is adorned with beautiful snow-clad mountains that make it the hottest destination for love couples. Further, it's not that the place is meant only for love birds; tourists seeking some thrill can also visit the city. Shimla is highly popular as a skiing destination and thus, entices adventurers from different parts of the globe. It is indeed the best winter destination to visit in India.. Spend a fun-filled day in the cultural hub of Shimla, Shimla Ridge. It's one of the best things to do here in Winter.

3. Andaman

Completely different from Goa, Andaman is a place which is tailor-made for those who are looking for relaxation in the lap of Mother Nature. It is one of the most stunning winter vacation destinations of India. The tranquillity of the place works as the main crowd puller. Besides serenity, travellers can enjoy exotic wildlife, especially the aquatic species. In addition to this, visitors can also indulge in snorkelling and other water sports, so as to add some thrill to their vacation.

4. Auli

One of the best winter tourist destinations in Uttarakhand, Auli is a home to various perfect skiing slopes. An adventure junky would never miss a chance to visit this place, as the skiing experience offered at Auli is simply matchless. Though the trip to Auli is short, the experience earned provides memories that last forever. If you want to enjoy the best of winter tourism in India then Auli is definitely a place to be in. Glittery snow-covered hill station of Auli offers the thrill of Skiing which is the best thing to do in winters.Trekking from Auli to Gorson Bugyal will enable you to witness the panoramic vistas of lush green meadows and picturesque Nanda Devi Peak.Camping is among the best things to do in Auli. A beautiful night of bonfire, delicious food and fun filled games will make your day.Cable car ride is one of the most popular attractions in Auli. Treat your eyes with stunning views of the town.
